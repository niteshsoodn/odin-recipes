Healthy Pizza

Description

An easy and healthy pizza.

Ingridients

Dough

  • 1dl greek yogurt
  • 2dl all-purpose flour

Toppings

  • 1dl Tomato sauce
  • An Onion
  • A Tomato
  • 100g Chicken
  • 30g Cheese

Steps

  1. Create pizza dough by mixing greek yogurt and all-purpose flour together
  2. Roll out the pizza dough
  3. Add the toppings
  4. Bake at 200 degrees for 15 minutes
